Letting us know a NHS pension member has died Have you used the 'Tell Us Once' service to notify about this bereavement?

Tell Us Once is a service that lets you report a bereavement to most government organisations in one go.

A registrar will explain the service when you register the death of the deceased.

They'll either:

  • complete the Tell Us Once service with you
  • give you a unique reference number so you can use the service yourself online or by phone
